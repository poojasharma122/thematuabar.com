About

A True Local in the Heart of Matua, Tauranga

The Matua Bar has been a fixture in Tauranga’s Matua neighbourhood for years, earning its reputation as a welcoming spot for locals to gather, relax, and enjoy good company. With a focus on quality drinks, lively events, and an atmosphere that feels like an extension of your own lounge, we’re proud to be a place where everyone can feel at ease.

From weekly pool tournaments and quiz nights to live music and pokies, The Matua Bar combines traditional pub spirit with a community-focused approach. Run by locals, supported by locals, and shaped by the stories and laughs shared here every week, our bar is more than just a venue—it’s part of Matua’s social fabric.

Whether you’re here for a quick drink after work, a full night out, or to host a celebration, The Matua Bar offers an authentic experience that blends Tauranga’s laid-back lifestyle with a lively and friendly environment.
