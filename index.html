The Matua Bar – A Neighbourhood Favourite in Tauranga

Good Drinks. Great People. Always Something On.

Tucked into the heart of Matua, The Matua Bar is the kind of place that feels like home after one visit. Cold pints, warm company, and an easygoing atmosphere that draws a local crowd seven days a week. Whether you’re catching the rugby, winding down after work, or heading out for live music on the weekend, this is the kind of bar that gets the mix just right.

What’s Behind the Bar

You’ll find a rotating tap of cold beers, New Zealand wines, solid spirits, and a few house cocktails we’ve perfected over time. We stock both the old favourites and a few indie labels worth discovering. Low and no-alcohol options are always on hand too—because everyone’s pace is different.

Daily specials and happy hours run throughout the week, including match-day offers and combo deals for events.

Host Your Next Event With Us

Whether it’s a birthday, team night out, family celebration, or just an excuse to round up the crew, The Matua Bar has flexible space for private functions. No hire fees. No minimums. Just good food, cold drinks, and the right setup.
