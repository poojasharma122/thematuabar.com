Party’s & Events

Host Your Event at The Matua Bar

Private Functions, Casual Celebrations, and Everything in Between

Looking for a laid-back, local spot to celebrate? The Matua Bar offers the perfect setting for birthdays, work parties, reunions, farewells, and casual get-togethers. No room hire fees. No strict packages. Just good drinks, great service, and a space that fits your vibe.

Flexible Spaces for Any Group

We’ve got a few different areas available depending on the size of your group:

Main Bar Area

Ideal for casual drinks, shared platters, and groups that want to stay in the heart of the action.

Side Bar Space

Semi-private and perfect for medium-sized groups. Great for speeches, slide shows, or just a bit more elbow room.

Pool Room

Great for smaller, relaxed gatherings with a bit of competition on the table.

Outdoor Courtyard

Weather-permitting, our courtyard is perfect for day sessions, BBQs, or casual catch-ups under the sun.

We’ve Hosted It All

  • 21sts, 30ths, 40ths and everything in between

  • Work functions and team break-ups

  • Family birthdays and anniversaries

  • Club meetups and fundraisers

  • Engagement parties and farewell drinks

  • Community groups and club nights like Sock Club socials
